⚠️
Underweight — BMI below 18.5
Less than 18.5
Risks
Nutrient deficiencies, weak immune system, fatigue, bone loss, hormonal issues
Diet
Increase calorie intake with nutrient-dense foods — nuts, dairy, eggs, lean meat, whole grains
Exercise
Focus on strength training to build muscle mass. Avoid excessive cardio
Goal
Gain 0.25–0.5 kg per week gradually through a calorie surplus
✅
Normal Weight — BMI 18.5–24.9
18.5 to 24.9
Status
You are in the healthy range. Lower risk of chronic disease
Diet
Maintain a balanced diet — fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean protein, healthy fats
Exercise
Mix of cardio and strength training 4–5 days/week to maintain fitness
Goal
Stay consistent. Focus on quality nutrition and sleep
⚡
Overweight — BMI 25–29.9
25 to 29.9
Risks
High blood pressure, type 2 diabetes risk, joint pain, sleep apnea
Diet
Reduce processed foods, sugar, and saturated fats. Eat more fiber and vegetables
Exercise
150+ minutes of cardio per week. Add strength training 2–3 days/week
Goal
Aim to lose 0.5–1 kg per week through a moderate calorie deficit
🚨
Obese — BMI 30 and above
30 or higher
Risks
Heart disease, stroke, diabetes, certain cancers, mobility issues
Diet
Work with a nutritionist. Cut sugar, alcohol, ultra-processed food. Eat whole foods
Exercise
Start with low-impact activity — walking, swimming, cycling. Build up gradually
Goal
Even losing 5–10% of body weight significantly reduces health risks
